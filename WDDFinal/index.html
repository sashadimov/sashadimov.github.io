Who am I really?
I’m a human who loves everything from oil painting to whipping up some damn good crème brûlée. I am a current student at UC Berkeley and I believe that our future lies in the hands of good (and terrible) design.
Passionate
about design, simplicity,
and everything in between.
I often find myself in three planes of existence. The first being in bed, the second standing on one leg (in a sort of a tree pose, it’s just a strange habit that I’ve always had) leaning against a kitchen counter vigorously whisking away at some merengue, and the third being on a wall that just happens to be dotted with colorful little holds that sometimes are just a bit too hard to hold on to. So sure, I sleep (a lot) and cook (a lot) and prefer to post myself in the climbing gym rather than torture myself on a treadmill (who wants to do that anyway?) And sure, I live in my grey hoodie but damn do I love design. Every single type of it. These little pieces of me show up in my work, whether it is a simple sketch or a full on app redesign. So please, go ahead, check out just a little of what I love to do.
